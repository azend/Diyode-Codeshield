Diyode Codeshield

An electronics tool for teaching programming first.

View project onGitHub

The Diyode Codeshield is a tool for teaching programming first. Built from the powerful and open source Arduino platform, the Codeshield is a great platform for people of any age to get started with electronics.

Getting your Codeshield

Unfortunately, Codeshields are currently not available for purchase. We are however selling some initial boards at various events and community activities we attend. If you'd really like to see an initial board, come out to Diyode Makers' Club and ask!
